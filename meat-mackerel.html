About Us

Meat & Mackerel is a premium seafood & grill restaurant by Calicut Paragon, located in the heart of Barsha Heights, Dubai. Our chefs blend the finest oceanic flavors with expertly aged prime cuts, delivering an unforgettable dining experience.

With a 4.8/5 rating from over 2,300 delighted guests, we pride ourselves on impeccable service, an elegant ambience, and a menu that celebrates both land and sea.
