Our Story

Founded by Chef Alessandro Rossi, GIA brings the soul of Italy to Dubai’s most vibrant district. Our philosophy blends traditional recipes with contemporary techniques, delivering an unforgettable dining experience.

Chef Alessandro, a Michelin‑starred veteran, curates a menu that celebrates regional diversity—from the rich ragù of Bologna to the delicate seafood of the Amalfi Coast.
